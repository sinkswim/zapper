Welcome to Zapper! Login or register if this is your first time playing.

The game consists of shooting incoming UFOs, going through increasing levels of difficulty, improving your score, and climbing the leaderboard.

The game ends if you fail to annihilate at least 70% of the opponents in each level or if you successfully complete the seventh and final level.

Enjoy! 🚀👽🕹
